GoGo's Events

Classes and Events

GoGo's skate part offers you a comprehensive selection of the finest programming options. From novice through pros, there’s a program here that’s a perfect fit for your interests and your schedule!

All of the programs and events are open to the public and do not require a membership at the park. So what are you waiting for? Sign up for a class today! Register Online!

Below are a few of the programs available through GoGo's Skate Park! Be sure to check our current activity guide and online registration site for many more!

Featured Events

Beginner Skateboarding Camp

GoGo's little one's offers indoor winter classes on the foundations and basics of skating.

Boardr Series Competition

All ages and skills skateboarding contest series that is designed to provide a fun and fulfilling skateboarding experience for all participants.

Technique and Tricks Workshop

Learn tips and tricks from GoGo's own award winning skaters!

Skate and Create: Art Meets Skating

Compete against grafitti artists for your chance to throw on our walls!

Limited Events

Partnering with Blick!

Come decorate a board for a chance to get featured in our artist gallery! Top 3 winners recieve a cash prize and certificate from Blick.

DGK Gear Day

DGK is setting up a pop-up shop for exclusive limited time 2024 GoGo Gear!

Orangatang Maintenance Series

Orangatang technicians stop by to do maintenance on your boards!

Sol Skate Miami Series

Skaters from the miami branch of Sol Skate stop by for an exchange of tricks and techniques!
